    Introduction

    My TOSHIBA Satelite T652 (Model No. PSPKAN-01700M) which was bought in 2011 have never turned on.
    Therefore, I decided to recycle it.
    Before doing so, I will try to dissabsemble TOSHIBA Satelite T652.

    Disassemble TOHISBA Satelite T652

    1. Flip it to the back side

    1. Remove a battery.

    1. Open the lid and remove all the screws.

    1. Place it with the keyboard side facing up.

    1. Gently pry up the edge with a screwdriver and remove the keyboard.

    Note 1:

    Be careful not to apply too much force, or it may crack as shown below.

    Note 2:

    Before removing the keyboard, lift it slightly and disconnect the cable.
